## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.sling.com.ng](https://app.sling.com.ng) and log in to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API token">
    Visit [Account → API](https://app.sling.com.ng/panel/account/api) in your dashboard to copy your API token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Make a `POST` request to `/api/v1/send-sms` with your token in the `Authorization` header and start messaging.
  </Step>
</Steps>
